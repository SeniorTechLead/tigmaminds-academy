# Gen-4 Video Prompts — 20 Mythology Stories

Use with Runway Gen-4. Upload the Midjourney/Runway card image as starting frame, paste the prompt below.

## Hindu (5)

### 1. Churning of the Ocean
`Cosmic ocean churning slowly, a massive mountain rotating as a giant serpent coils around it, colorful liquid layers separating — golden nectar rising to the surface while dark poison sinks below, figures pulling rhythmically on either side, slow majestic camera push in, cinematic, 5 seconds`

### 2. Hanuman Lifted a Mountain
`A powerful warrior figure soaring through clouds at night carrying a glowing mountain peak, herbs on the mountain shimmering with golden light, clouds parting as he flies past, camera tracking alongside, starlit sky, cinematic, 5 seconds`

### 3. Ravana's Ten Heads
`A crowned king seated on a golden throne, camera slowly orbiting around him, his ten heads each contemplating different scrolls and instruments, subtle golden energy flickering between the heads, ornate palace interior, warm lighting, 5 seconds`

### 4. Pushpaka Vimana
`A golden ornate flying chariot gliding smoothly through clouds above ancient temple spires, camera following from below looking up, wind streaming past intricate carvings, sunlight catching the gold surface, birds flying alongside, 5 seconds`

### 5. Agni — Science of Fire
`Sacred flames on a stone altar shifting colors — deep orange to vivid blue to emerald green to violet — smoke curling upward into a dark sky filled with stars, embers drifting gently, warm flickering light illuminating carved temple walls, 5 seconds`

## Buddhist (5)

### 6. Sand Mandala
`Overhead shot looking down, a monk's hand slowly pouring colored sand through a metal funnel onto a dark stone surface, intricate geometric patterns emerging line by line, grains of red blue and gold sand falling precisely, camera slowly zooming out to reveal the growing circular design, 5 seconds`

### 7. Bodhi Tree
`Gentle breeze moving through the heart-shaped leaves of a massive ancient tree, golden afternoon light dappling through the canopy onto a still figure meditating below, leaves slowly falling one by one, camera pulling back to reveal the enormous spreading tree, 5 seconds`

### 8. Wheel of Dharma
`A magnificent golden eight-spoked wheel slowly spinning atop an ancient stone pillar, warm sunrise light catching each spoke as it turns, deer grazing peacefully in a misty park below, camera slowly rising to be level with the spinning wheel, 5 seconds`

### 9. Monastery Bells of Tawang
`A monk striking a large bronze bell in a stone archway high in the mountains, the bell visibly vibrating, mist drifting across a snow-covered valley below, sound waves subtly rippling outward through the cold air, camera pulling back to show the monastery, 5 seconds`

### 10. Angulimala
`A kneeling warrior figure, dark armor slowly dissolving and transforming into peaceful monk robes, the color palette shifting from dark reds and blacks to warm gold and white, expression softening, light growing brighter around him, slow cinematic transformation, 5 seconds`

## Christian (5)

### 11. David and Goliath
`A shepherd boy spinning a leather sling overhead in circular motion against a dramatic sky, the spinning stone a blur, then the release — the stone flying in a long arc across a sunlit valley toward a distant armored figure, slow motion, 5 seconds`

### 12. Noah's Ark
`A massive wooden ark floating on dark churning ocean waters, rain falling heavily, pairs of animals walking up a wide ramp — elephants, giraffes, doves flying overhead, camera slowly rising to reveal the enormous scale of the ship against towering storm clouds, a faint rainbow forming, 5 seconds`

### 13. Star of Bethlehem
`Three figures on camels crossing sand dunes at night, an intensely bright star slowly moving across a vast starry sky ahead of them, sand rippling gently in the wind, long shadows stretching behind, camera tracking alongside the caravan, deep blue night, 5 seconds`

### 14. Parting of the Red Sea
`Ocean waters slowly parting, camera at ground level looking through the widening gap, massive walls of blue-green water rising on either side revealing sand and coral on the seabed, wind blowing through, people beginning to walk on the dry path, golden desert light, 5 seconds`

### 15. Tower of Babel
`Camera slowly tilting upward along a massive ancient stepped tower reaching into clouds, workers visible on scaffolding at different levels, clouds swirling around the top of the tower, birds circling, dramatic sense of scale, warm light on brick and stone, 5 seconds`

## Islamic (5)

### 16. Geometry of the Alhambra
`Camera slowly gliding through an arched corridor of a grand palace, incredibly intricate geometric tile patterns on every surface catching golden afternoon light streaming through the arches, shadows of the arches moving slowly across the tiled floor, dust motes floating in sunbeams, 5 seconds`

### 17. The Astrolabe
`A beautifully crafted brass astrolabe slowly rotating in a scholar's hands against a clear starry night sky, star positions aligning with the fine etchings on the polished brass disk, warm candlelight reflecting off the golden metal surface, 5 seconds`

### 18. Al-Khwarizmi and Algebra
`A scholar's hand writing mathematical equations on parchment with a reed pen, dark ink flowing smoothly, a brass balance scale on the desk gently tipping as geometric shapes are placed on each side, candlelight flickering across stacked ancient books, warm amber tones, 5 seconds`

### 19. Well of Zamzam
`Water gushing up powerfully from dry cracked desert ground, forming a clear spring that flows into a stone well, camera slowly pulling back to reveal a woman and child in a vast desert landscape, distant mountains on the horizon, golden desert light, 5 seconds`

### 20. Muezzin's Call
`Sunset over a medieval city of domes and minarets, camera slowly rising up the side of a tall ornate minaret, reaching the top where a robed figure stands with arms raised, the golden sun setting behind the city skyline, warm amber and deep blue tones, 5 seconds`

## Tips for Gen-4
- Upload the card image as "Image Input" (starting frame)
- Paste the prompt as "Text Input"
- Set duration to 5 seconds
- Camera motion keywords that work well: "camera slowly", "push in", "pull back", "tracking alongside", "rising", "orbiting"
- For loops: add "seamless loop" but Gen-4 doesn't always nail this
- If motion is too wild: add "subtle motion", "gentle", "slow"
- If too static: add "dynamic", "flowing", "cinematic movement"
